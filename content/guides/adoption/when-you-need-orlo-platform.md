# When You Need Orlo Platform

Use Orlo Platform when:

- you need multi-tenant isolation
- you need the full evaluation-to-deployment loop
- you need retrieval, feedback promotion, and deployment state in one system
- you need approval queues, policy persistence, and governed agent sessions
- you want non-technical or semi-technical teams to use the system directly

If your goal is operational AI, not just reusable code, this is usually the right surface.
